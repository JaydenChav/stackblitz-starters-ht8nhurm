About Book Haven

Book Haven Bookstore is a local treasure for book enthusiasts, offering a diverse selection of books, magazines, journals, and office supplies. With a deep-rooted commitment to fostering literary exploration and a love for reading, we are dedicated to creating an inviting space that encourages literary discovery and supports intellectual growth.

Our mission is to amplify visibility for great literature, cultivate a vibrant literary community, and simplify the process of book inquiries and purchases. We serve a broad audience of book lovers and knowledge seekers, including avid readers, students, and individuals in search of high-quality reading materials and office essentials.

Our Values

We believe in the transformative power of books and are committed to:

  • Promoting literacy and lifelong learning
  • Supporting local authors and publishers
  • Creating an inclusive community space
  • Providing exceptional customer service

Location Details

We're conveniently located in the heart of downtown, with easy parking available both on the street and in the nearby public parking garage. Our store is wheelchair accessible and we welcome service animals.

Public transportation options include Bus Routes 15, 22, and 34, all with stops within two blocks of our store.
